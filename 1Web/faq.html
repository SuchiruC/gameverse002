Frequently Asked Questions

Here you'll find answers to common questions about our products, services, and policies. Whether you're curious about delivery times, warranties, or trade-in options, we've got you covered!

Do you offer warranties for gaming consoles and peripherals?

Yes, all our gaming consoles and peripherals come with a standard manufacturer warranty, typically lasting 1 year. Extended warranties are also available for purchase at an additional cost.

Are pre-orders available for upcoming games and consoles?

Absolutely! You can pre-order upcoming games, consoles, and accessories through our website. Pre-orders are secured with a small deposit, and you'll be notified once your item is ready to ship.

Do you offer trade-in options for old consoles or games?

Yes, we have a trade-in program for select consoles, games, and accessories. Bring your items to our store or contact us online for a valuation. Trade-in credit can be used toward your next purchase.
